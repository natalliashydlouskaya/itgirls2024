Flower Care Tips:
	Keep flowers in a clean vase filled with fresh water.
    Trim stems at an angle before placing in water.
    Change water and recut stems every few days.
	Avoid placing flowers in direct sunlight or near heat sources.
	Remove any wilting or dead blooms to prolong freshness.
